What Blog Auto-Share does

Blog Auto-Share is a personal, single-user tool. Its sole function is to share links to my own published blog posts to my own LinkedIn profile, with my authorization. It does not read feeds, connections, messages, or profile data beyond the minimum needed to publish a post on my behalf.

No user data is stored, sold, or shared with anyone. Authentication credentials for Blog Auto-Share are held privately by me and are never published or transmitted to third parties.
